GitHub is looking for an engineer to join our analytics team. This team is focused on building infrastructure to leverage the GitHub data set as a whole--both in service of evaluating changes to the product and exploring new product areas. We are looking for someone excited about the construction and operation of systems that turn large data streams into usable information.

Responsibilities:

    • Quickly write scripts and sketches to evaluate an idea, answer questions, or prove a concept
    • Turn these ideas into real production services
    • Work with teammates to design services and API contracts
    • Refine and tune production services over time to improve throughput, resource utilization, performance, and other factors as necessary
    • Build tooling necessary to help both your immediate team and others understand the runtime behavior of production services
    • Seek and offer feedback on code and design choices
    • Maintain and extend our data collection, storage, and processing infrastructure

Qualifications:

    • Strong written and verbal communication skills
    • Strong computer science fundamentals (data structures and algorithms)
    • Strong understanding of distributed systems
    • Proficiency in one or more scripting languages
    • Proficiency in one or more typed programming languages
    • Understanding of relational database technology and query planning (e.g. MySQL, Postgres, Redshift, Hive, Spark SQL, etc.)
    • Understanding of efficient computation en masse (e.g. Hadoop, Tez, Kinesis, Samza, etc.)

Location:

    • Position needs to be based in one of our hubs: Bay Area; Boulder, CO; or Portland, OR
